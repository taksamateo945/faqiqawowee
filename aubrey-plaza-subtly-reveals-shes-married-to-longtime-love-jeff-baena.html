Aubrey Plaza Subtly Reveals She's Married to Longtime Love Jeff Baena!

Aubrey Plaza just revealed some very happy news in the most subtle way!

The 36-year-old Parks and Recreation actress is married to her longtime love Jeff Baena and she told the world about the marriage in a new Instagram post.

Click inside to see what Aubrey Plaza put on her page…

Aubrey dropped the wedding news while praising Jeff in a post about their upcoming movie Spin Me Round, which will begin production next month in Italy.

“So proud of my darling husband @jeffbaena for dreaming up another film that takes us to italia to cause some more trouble ?,” Aubrey wrote in the post. Her rep later confirmed to People that they’re indeed married.

Jeff is directing the upcoming movie, which will star Aubrey alongside Alison Brie, Alessandro Nivola, Molly Shannon, and Lil Rel Howery. The film was co-written by Jeff and Alison, according to Deadline.

In the movie, Alison “plays the manager of the Bakersfield, California franchise of the Italian chain eatery Tuscan Grove. She wins an all-expenses trip to the company’s gorgeous ‘institute’ outside of Florence, Italy, and the chance to meet the restaurant chain’s wealthy and charismatic owner (Nivola). Along with a mismatched group of local American franchise managers, Amber finds a far different adventure than the romantic fantasy she had imagined.”

Aubrey and Jeff have been dating for the past ten years and she has starred in his movies Life After Beth and The Little Hours. She recently opened up about their relationship in a new interview!
