How Old is John Luke Robertson?

John Luke Robertson is currently 28 years old. He was born to his parents, Korrie Brigitte and Willie Robertson, on October 11, 1995. Born into a big family of seven, John is the oldest of 6 Robertson children.

He was born in Lynchburg, Virginia, the United States, and spent the bulk of his childhood there with his parents and five siblings; Bella, Rebecca, Rowdy, Sadie, and Willie Alexander Robertson.

He is an Author and Public Speaker

Following the robust accolades John got from the television community, many of his fans assumed that he would follow in his parents’ and grandparents’ footsteps by pursuing a full-time career in the industry. However, he went solo on his career journey and decided to venture into writing. Judging by his recent success, this was a good decision as it has enabled him to infuse his love for God and kids into the various children’s books he has written over the years.  

He also devotes a great deal of his time to various Christian outreach missions around the world, speaking passionately about God and faith. He focuses more on teaching people about the importance of living life to the fullest.

Also known as Young and Beardless, John currently has over six published books, which are filled with details drawn from members of the Robertson family and their various antics. His motivational and children’s books have been well-welcomed by his readers and some of them include:

John is a Businessman

Although he didn’t stick to the typical Robertson family business, John is easily one of the most successful Robertson family members. While growing up, he worked at Camp Ch-Yo-Ca, a Christian youth camp founded by his grandfather, Phil Robertson, and is presently the director of the Ch-Yo-Ca non-profit organization.

In 2019, he founded Railway Coffee, a unique coffee shop located on Park Avenue, Louisiana. Since it was created, Railway Coffee has experienced steady growth and is well appreciated by coffee lovers. It currently has about three locations across Ruston.

Their marriage was a lovely, charming event with close friends and family in attendance. And although they were barely past their teenage years when they got married, John and Mary have an amazingly loving and romantic relationship. They have been married for about 53 years now.
