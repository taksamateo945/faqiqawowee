Eric Andre Ate His Vomit in Front of Lauren Conrad: How Did She React?

This interview is dunzo! Comedian Eric Andre is known for his over-the-top, fake interviews on Adult Swim’s The Eric Andre Show. In a new interview with HuffPost Live, the Don’t Trust the B— in Apartment 23 alum, 31, revealed that he had a particularly disgusting surprise for an unsuspecting Lauren Conrad on the upcoming season of the comedy show. 

“I puked during her interview, and then I ate my puke back up and she left,” the fake talk show host said laughing. “I tried to be like ‘Hey it’s all in jest, and I’m just playing a wacky guy,’ but she had her sunglasses on, and she was ready to leave the building as soon as possible.” 

In previous seasons of the show, Andre has given his guests a warning about his crazy antics, but this season he tried a different approach.

“This year I tried to tell them absolutely nothing,” he said. “I’m always prepping people, but I was like ‘What if I don’t even say a single word to them before or after?’”
