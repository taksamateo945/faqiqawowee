Kelly Clarkson Announces 2020 Las Vegas Residency

Talk show host, The Voice judge, American Idol season 1 Winner, Grammy Award Winner Kelly Clarkson has announced today on her talk show, The Kelly Clarkson Show that she will be having a Las Vegas Residency starting April 1st, 2020.

The Because of You singer will perform all her iconic hits from “Since U Been Gone” to “Stronger” to “Love So Soft” and more! For the last couple decades, Kelly has been everyone’s America’s Sweetheart, she is literally doing everything from talk show host, to judge, to the singer, she might as well be the new Ryan Seacrest.

Kelly has announced that her residency, Kelly Clarkson: Invincible, will start on April 1st at Zappos Theater at Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino.

To celebrate the announcement,  Kelly performed a special Kellyoke medley on her talk show.
