Brenda Jean Bakke (born May 15th 1963) is an American actor and real estate agent. She is most famous for her roles in films from the 1990s like Hot Shots! Part Deux, Gunmen, Demon Knight and Under Siege 2: Dark Territory. Bakke also was Lana Turner in the 1997 Neo-noir L.A. Confidential.

In the 2000s, Bakke continued appearing in movies, playing roles in lesser-known films like The Quickie and Moving August. The following years saw her acting as guest stars in the episodes CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, NYPD Blue, The Mentalist and Supernatural. The year 2015 saw her was cast in the role of Virginia on The Oprah Winfrey Network prime time soap drama When Loving You Is Wrong. In 2016 Bakke played guest-starring parts in the episodes Heartbeat as well as Criminal Minds: Beyond Borders.
