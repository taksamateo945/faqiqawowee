Griselda Siciliani in Bathing Suit Says &quot;Good Morning&quot; Celebwell

Griselda Siciliani, the Argentine actress currently starring in the Netflix hit Bardo, False Chronicle of a Handful of Truths, is enjoying a vacation right now. And she just shared some highlights for her followers on Instagram. In some of the photos, she wore a yellow bathing suit with white polka dots. She captioned the post, "Buen dia para tutti!" How does she stay so fit? Read on to see 5 ways Griselda Siciliani stays in shape and the photos that prove they work—and to get beach-ready yourself, don't miss these essential 30 Best-Ever Celebrity Bathing Suit Photos!

Siciliani lives an active life. And she doesn't always do things alone. She likes to do activities with her friends and family. Siciliani posted this video on Instagram of herself and others playing soccer on the beach. She captioned it, "Fulbito. Playa. Atardecer. Y mi familia."

Siciliani loves to dance as a way of staying in shape. She does a lot of projects that require her to learn choreography. Siciliani did live dance performances, and posted highlights on Instagram. She shared this video advertising the event. Siciliani also shared this video of herself rehearsing in the studio, captioning it, "Bang bang."

Siciliani loves to spend time in the water. Swimming is one of the exercises she likes to do to stay in shape. Siciliani shared this photo on Instagram of herself floating on her back in Uruguay. She captioned it, "And whatever it may be…" She also shared this photo of herself in the water from the same trip. She captioned it, "Happy Valentine's Day."

Siciliani likes to jump rope as a way of staying in shape. She shared this video of herself doing so on Instagram. In it, she is seen moving in different directions as she jumps. Siciliani captioned it, "Dale Rocky! Here you train with the best of all." Jumping rope is a great form of cardio and aerobic exercise. 

Siciliani makes sure to eat healthy. She also makes sure to buy fresh food. Siciliani likes to buy food from farmer's markets. She shared this photo of herself at a fruit stand in Manhattan on Instagram. She captioned it, "Strawberry fields forever." Buying food at farmer's markets isn't just a way to find fresh food, it also helps support local businesses and vendors.
