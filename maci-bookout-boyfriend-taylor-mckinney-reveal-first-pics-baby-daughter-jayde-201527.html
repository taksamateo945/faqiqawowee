Maci Bookout, Boyfriend Reveal First Pics of Baby Daughter Jayde

Here she is! Teen Mom OG stars Maci Bookout and her boyfriend, Taylor McKinney, welcomed their daughter, Jayde Carter, on May 29. But the pair didn’t shared the first photos of their newest addition with fans until Thursday, July 2. 

“who wants to see a picture of baby Jayde?” Bookout, 23, teased via Twitter before posting the first pic. 

“Introducing the prettiest girl in the world! Jayde Carter McKinney @tmon3yyy | A HUGE thank you to the photographer @brittanyphotographs for turning such beautiful moments into amazing art for our family!” the reality star captioned the professional photo with her longtime love cradling their daughter via Instagram. 

Bookout posted a second image of her son, Bentley, 6, whom she shares with ex Ryan Edwards, kissing his baby sister’s head, writing, “heartstrings @brittanyphotographs.”
