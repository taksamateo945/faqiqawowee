Comedian Fuquan Johnson among 3 dead &amp; model Kate Quigley in critical condition after taking lace

COMEDIAN Fuquan Johnson has been found dead after an apparent drug overdose, according to reports.

The Los Angeles-based comic had been at a party in the Venice neighborhood on Friday night where he was pronounced dead shortly after midnight, TMZ reports.

Two other people, who have not yet been named, were also found dead.

A fourth, named by TMZ as comedian and model Kate Quigley, is reportedly in hospital in a critical condition.
