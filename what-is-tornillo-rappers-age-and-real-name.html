What is Tornilla Rapper’s Real Name?

The rap artist was given the full name Osiel Armando Vazquez Perez at birth. However, he chose to go with the moniker, Tornillo after he launched his music and social media career.

Talking about his reasons for choosing Tornillo as his stage name, the young Mexican rapper said it is interpreted as screw in English and he chose it to pay an emotional homage to the profession of his father.

He said he will forever cherish his childhood memories when he would assist his dad in his mechanic workshop, all the practical skills he gained, and the understanding of the principle of diligent work

It was during his days at the carpentry workshop that Tornillo’s bond with his father was cemented for life and his onstage persona of Tornillo Vazquez started taking shape
