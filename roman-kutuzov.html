Career

MR. Roman Kutuzov was promoted from Soldier to Colonel earlier this year. He headed the Russian Airborne Forces’ 38th Separate Communications Regiment as a Colonel (military unit 54164.) Then, in 2017, he was elevated once again and took over as acting commander of the 5th Combined Arms Army. After two years as a commander, he was named Interim Commander of the 29th Combined Arms Army.

In 2020, Roman Kutuzov remained Chief of Staff of the 29th Combined Arms Army. He was a Major General at the time of his death on June 5, 2022. During the struggle for Sievierodonetsk–Lysychansk in Lysychansk, Ukraine, General Roman Kutuzov was killed in the hamlet of Mykolaivka. He was leading the Donetsk People’s Republic’s 1st Army Corps when he died at Mykolaivka. During his military service, Roman Kutuzov received several medals and distinctions.
