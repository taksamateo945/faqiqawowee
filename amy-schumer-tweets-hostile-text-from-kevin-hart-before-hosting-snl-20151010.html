Amy Schumer Tweets &quot;Hostile&quot; Text From Kevin Hart Before Hosting SNL

Amy Schumer tweeted what she called a “hostile text” she received from Kevin Hart on Saturday morning prior to hosting the Oct. 10 episode of Saturday Night Live.

“Hey I know your hosting SNL tonight… If you get nervous during your monologue just tell the audience that you know me,” Hart texted. “I promise it will help.”

Hart knows a thing or two about hosting the NBC show, as he previously hosted earlier this year on Jan. 17. His text message continued:

“Or you can FaceTime me but I probably wouldn’t be able to pick up because I’m busy doing stuff. Just spit balling here… I’m trying to help… you know what f–k it… you will be fine… I hope.. God bless…K Hart out.”

The Trainwreck star, in response, shared the photo of his text and jokingly tweeted, “Hostile text @KevinHart4real #snl.”

Saturday night’s episode will be the Inside Amy Schumer star’s first time hosting the show.
