BTS Suga's 'Agust D' Mixtape Claims #1 Spot on Worldwide iTunes Album Chart

As of February 23, BTS member Suga’s mixtape, Agust D, has hit the #1 spot on the Worldwide iTunes Album Chart, surpassing other hit records such as ÷ (Deluxe), The Greatest Showman, Black Panther The Album and more.

Originally released on Soundcloud in 2016, Agust D has since been made available for streaming and purchase on both Spotify and iTunes. On Spotify, the mixtape accumulated over 4.5 million listens in less than 24 hours of its February 22 release date.

Listen to ‘Agust D’ below.

Under the pseudonym Agust D, Suga is the second member of BTS to release a mixtape, following leader RM’s self-titled release in 2015. J-Hope is also expecting to release his own mixtape, dubbed the “Hixtape” by fans, on March 2nd. To tease fans about the upcoming release, J-Hope shared a clip of himself working on the project.
