It's a Boy!Criminal Minds Star A.J. Cook Welcomes Baby No. 2

It's a boy for A.J. Cook and her husband, Nathan Andersen!

The Criminal Minds star, 37, gave birth to the couple's second baby, a son named Phoenix Sky Andersen, on Thursday, July 23, her rep confirmed to People.

Little Phoenix — who weighed 7 lbs., 6 oz., and measured 20.5 inches — joins older brother Mekhai Allan Andersen, 6.
