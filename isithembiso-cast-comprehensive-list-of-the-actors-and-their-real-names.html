Comprehensive List of the Actors and their Real Names

Created and produced by Bomb Productions as a modern-day reimagining of the classic Little Red Riding Hood fairytale, Isithembiso is a South African soap opera that premiered on Mzansi Magic on April 3, 2017. The telenovela ran for three seasons on the TV station before getting canceled in early 2020. As a result, its final episode was aired on March 27, 2020. Isithembiso follows the lives of two young people, who moved from rural SA to the City of Gold in search of better life opportunities, but eventually realized that all that glitters is not gold.

The series, which is centered on a millennial migrant story, delves into the lives of South African youths, exploring their ambitions, choices, and consequences. The two main characters on the show were played by SA film industry newbies, Zamani Mbatha and Nandi Mbatha. Isithembiso cast members also include veteran actors like Mavuso Magabane, Nandi Nyembe, Lillian Dube, and Hamilton Dlamini.

    1. Hamilton Dlamini

    • Role: Banzi Kuene
    • Age: 54 years old
    • Cast Type: Main
    • Number of Episodes: Over 200 episodes

    Hamilton Dlamini sensationally portrayed the character of ruthless and manipulative Banzi Kuene, who was nicknamed “The Wolf of Braamfontein”. He was one of the main cast members of Isithembiso and thus, made appearances in seasons 1, 2, and 3. Over the years, the 54-year-old actor has made a name for himself in the film industry with appearances in several TV series. He is known for playing a leading role in the SABC1 mini-series adaptation of Shakespeare’s King Lear titled, Izingane zoBaba. In the year 2005, Dlamini starred in the SABC1 TV series, Mzee wa Two Six.

    Later in 2009, he scored another leading role in Stokvel, appearing in the sixth season of the series. He subsequently played the role of a private investigator, TT in the Mzansi Magic comedy series, Boomba and TT in 2012. He further featured as Mnqobi Simelane in the e.tv soap opera, Umilo in the year 2015.

    2. Mxolisi Masongo

    • Role: Leo
    • Age: 34 years old
    • Cast Type: Main
    • Number of Episodes: Over 150 episodes

    South African actor, Mxolisi Masongo portrayed the character of Leo in the Mzansi Magic soap opera. He starred in the three seasons of Isithembiso and garnered much popularity for his performances in the TV series. Apart from being an actor, Masongo also works in the capacity of a journalist and presenter. He was born in Leandra, Mpumalanga on December 21, 1989, and began his career in entertainment when he was 18 years old. He started out as a dancer, joining a dance group known as Phly Boyz. He later worked as a puppeteer and manager of the 2010 FIFA World Cup mascot, Zakumi.

    As a journalist, Mxolisi Masongo has been associated with multiple TV stations such as SABC1, Turkish TV channel, TRT and Arise TV in London. He served as the host of Sunday Live and Expressions on SABC1. He then began his acting career in the year 2012 with a minor role in the third season of the SABC1 TV series, Tshisa. He moved on to land a starring role in isiPantsula which aired on Mzansi Magic in the next year, as well as a recurring role in Generations: The Legacy in 2016.

She is 42 years old and her date of birth is September 14th, 1981. Some of her acting credits include Backstage, Generations, and Tshisa.
